About Us

Veteran Masters Badminton Shuttle Associaton

Established in 1998, Kester Golf Club has stood as a testament to the enduring allure of the sport of golf. What began as a shared vision among a group of golf enthusiasts has evolved into a distinguished golfing haven that we are proud to offer to our members and guests today. Over the years, we've witnessed the growth of our club into a vibrant community bound together by the love of the game and a commitment to excellence.
